Who we are

More than a handout — a hand held

Hiding Plac3 is rooted in the belief that every person deserves sanctuary, dignity, and the love of Christ. We reach rough sleepers in our community with practical care, a listening ear, and the good news.

From Restoration Packages at Christmas, Easter, and throughout the year — ongoing follow-up — we walk alongside people, not past them. Our community extends to neighbours too, because need has no postcode.

We dream of expanding into a fully-equipped church-backed outreach community, complete with a mobile van hub, so we can meet people exactly where they are.

Our Four Pillars

What we stand on

Everything we do flows from four founding commitments — to provide sanctuary, nurture hope, offer real guidance, and celebrate freedom in community.

🕊️

Sanctuary

A cosy, spiritual reflection space where people can rest, recharge, and feel genuinely safe — physically and spiritually.

🌱

Hope

A kindness-based loyalty scheme and our Horizon Wall — filled with testimonies and opportunities — to remind people that tomorrow can look different.

🧭

Guidance

Informal advocacy, trauma-informed support, and purpose discovery, helping people navigate systems and find their footing again.

🎨

Freedom

Dietary inclusion and creative expression through Wednesday Witness — because everyone deserves a seat at the table and a voice worth hearing.
